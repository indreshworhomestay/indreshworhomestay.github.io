Authentic Experiences

Cooking Experience

At our homestay, we believe in offering more than just accommodation – we strive to provide enriching experiences that immerse our guests in the local culture.

We offer complimentary cooking classes where guests learn to prepare authentic Nepali dishes. Guests actively participate in every step, from selecting fresh ingredients to mastering traditional cooking techniques.

Through this hands-on experience, guests gain a deeper appreciation for Nepali culture while forming meaningful connections that go beyond hospitality.

Sightseeing Tours

Explore the rich heritage and spiritual sanctuaries of Panauti with our complimentary sightseeing tours.

Visit Namobuddha, Sorakhutte Pati, the ancient Indreshwor Temple, Triveni Ghat, and Gorakhnath Temple while discovering hidden cultural gems.

Each step is a journey into the heart of Nepalese history, spirituality, and everyday local life.
