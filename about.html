About Indreshwor Homestay

Welcome to our family-run homestay nestled in the heart of Panauti. With four cozy rooms and a deep commitment to hospitality, we invite you to experience the warmth of our home as your own.

Immerse yourself in the rich culture of Panauti with complimentary sightseeing tours, and savor the flavors of local cuisine through our complimentary cooking classes.

Let us be your gateway to authentic experiences and cherished memories in this enchanting corner of Nepal. Our homestay prides itself on being a serene sanctuary away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Whether you’re seeking a peaceful getaway or eager to explore the hidden gems of the area, Indreshwor Homestay promises a memorable stay filled with warmth, comfort, and genuine Nepali hospitality.
