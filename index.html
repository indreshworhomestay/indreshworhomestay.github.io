Explore the rich heritage and spiritual sanctuaries of Panauti with our complimentary sightseeing tours. Begin your journey at the house of Namobuddha then at Sorakhutte Pati, a charming square brimming with local culture offering a glimpse into the daily rhythms of Panauti life. Continue your pilgrimage at the ancient Indreshwor Temple, a masterpiece of Newari architecture dedicated to Lord Shiva, where echoes of devotion resonate through its intricately carved pillars and pagoda-style roofs. Triveni Ghat, where the converging rivers hold sacred significance in Hindu mythology, offering a serene spot for reflection and reverence.
