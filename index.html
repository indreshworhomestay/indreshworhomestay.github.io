About Us

Welcome to our family-run homestay nestled in the heart of Panauti. With four cozy rooms and a commitment to hospitality, we invite you to experience the warmth of our home as your own. Immerse yourself in the rich culture of Panauti with complimentary sightseeing tours, and savor the flavors of local cuisine with complimentary cooking classes. Our homestay is a serene sanctuary away from the hustle and bustle of city life.
