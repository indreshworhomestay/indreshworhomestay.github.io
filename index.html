About Us

Welcome to our family-run homestay nestled in the heart of Panauti. With four cozy rooms and a commitment to hospitality, we invite you to experience the warmth of our home as your own. Immerse yourself in the rich culture of Panauti with complimentary sightseeing tours, and savor the flavors of local cuisine with complimentary cooking classes. Let us be your gateway to authentic experiences and cherished memories in this enchanting corner of Nepal. Our homestay prides itself on being a serene sanctuary away from the hustle and bustle of city life. \Whether you’re seeking a peaceful getaway or eager to explore the hidden gems of the area, our homestay promises a memorable stay filled with warmth and hospitality.

FAQs

Do I need to pay for the total cost of accommodation at the time of booking?

No, you do not. You can pay after visiting the homestay.

How do I find the accommodation?

You can find us on Maps (Indreshwor Homestay) or follow directions from the local bus park as described.

Does the accommodation have Wi-Fi?

Yes, we have free Wi-Fi available.

Is it possible to use the kitchen?

Yes, the kitchen is available with induction and gas stove.

What type of breakfast is provided?

Typical Nepali breakfast is provided.
