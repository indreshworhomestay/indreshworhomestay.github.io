About Us

Welcome to our family-run homestay nestled in the heart of Panauti. With four cozy rooms and a commitment to heartfelt hospitality, we invite you to experience the warmth of our home as your own.

Our homestay is a serene sanctuary away from the bustle of city life, offering authentic local experiences, complimentary sightseeing tours, and hands-on Nepali cooking classes that create lasting memories.
